Software Engineering Traineeship Program 2024

Launch Your Career with AB InBev: Software Engineering Traineeship Program

What is it?

Our Software Engineering Traineeship (SET) programme is a 12-month program starting in September 2024. As a junior talent, you will work alongside experienced software engineers, learning best practices, developing code, and contributing to real-world projects. You will receive mentorship, training, and exposure to various technologies, frameworks, and methodologies.

You will be involved in the full software development lifecycle, from requirements gathering to deployment and maintenance. This program will equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to become a successful software engineer at AB InBev.

Who are we looking for?

We are looking for individuals passionate about software development, with a strong foundation in programming and problem-solving. You should be eager to learn, adaptable to new technologies, and able to work well in a team environment.

Key qualities we value:

  • Passionate about coding
  • Quick learner
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Good communication skills
  • Collaborative mindset

To be eligible:

  • Recent female graduate with a degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field
  • No more than 1 year of professional experience
  • Fluent in English
  • Eligible to work in the country of application

How can you apply?

Visit our careers page at Careers page to apply for the Software Engineering Traineeship Program. Make sure to submit all required documents and information.

Good to know

  • Successful completion of the program may lead to a full-time software engineering position at AB InBev
  • Opportunities for further career development and growth within the company
  • Competitive compensation package and benefits
  • Training and support provided throughout the program
